How it works
01
Login
Opens a browser. You log into Instagram manually. Your credentials are never stored or transmitted.
02
Scan
Auto-detects your account and fetches your followers and following lists via Instagram's API.
03
Review
See everyone who doesn't follow you back. Uncheck influencers, brands, or anyone you want to keep.
04
Unfollow
Removes selected accounts with built-in rate limiting to avoid Instagram action blocks.
