THE THESIS

Culture isn't something we have.

It's something we do.

and the house feels best when we're doing it together, on the same timing.

🏕️
🎲
retreat
/rəˈtriːt/
verb.
"to go somewhere as a house and come back tighter than we left."
The problem:
  • Last retreat = no agenda = everyone doing their own thing
What worked:
  • Squid Games. Whole org playing together. That's the blueprint.
The fix:
  • Every retreat gets a theme (Gambling Night, Y2K, Harry Potter — sorted into houses, inter-house competition all weekend)
  • Full agenda — games, competitions, meals, moshes, all on the same clock
  • Everyone moves together. Nobody drifts.
  • Mosh closes every night — I got the decks. 🎧
🍸
👯
mix
/mɪks/
verb.
"to put the house in a room with people who aren't us and let chemistry do its thing."
  • 1 mixer per month, minimum — committed 🤝
  • More frats, more sororities, more orgs on the roster
  • Beyond house parties — activity mixers, day events, pregame collabs
  • If our culture is worth having, it's worth exporting 🌍

  • Half of us already go out together. I'm making it official. 📋
