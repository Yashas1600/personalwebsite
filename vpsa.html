THE THESIS

Culture isn't something we have.

It's something we do.

and the frat feels best when we're doing it together, on the same timing.

retreat
/rəˈtriːt/
verb.
"to go somewhere as a frat and come back tighter than we left."
The problem:
  • Last retreat = no agenda = everyone doing their own thing
What worked:
  • Squid Games. Whole org playing together. That's the blueprint.
The fix:
  • Every retreat gets a theme (Gambling Night, Fight Night, Harry Potter — sorted into houses, inter-house competition all weekend)
  • We will have Fight Night and make every retreat better from now on
  • Full agenda — games, competitions, meals, moshes, all on the same clock
  • Everyone moves together. Nobody drifts.
  • Mosh closes every night — I got the decks. 🎧
